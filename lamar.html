About Lamar

Lamar Wilson works in a wide range of media and styles which displays the vast opportunities available to students/members of the fire arts center of chicago. Creating many pieces of furniture, jewelry, and sculpture, Lamar embodies the fire arts motto, of more work and less talk. Sign up for classes today and see what is available.
