Galleries

Well the new gallery is up and running. As planned it is a more efficient and user-friendly gallery. It will also make it easier for us to keep the gallery images more up-to-date.

While the gallery is not 100% finished, we have a good start on it. Please feel free to go check it out with the understanding that more Fire Arts member galleries are coming soon. In addition we hope to add many more images showing student works created in one of the many Fire Arts Center of Chicago classes. Be sure to check out our long list of classes including armor forging, metal sculpture, bronze casting as well as other foundry classes, figure sculpture and drawing, stone carving, and more to come.

Once you decide what you want to do, stop by our registration page to sign up for classes. We apologize for the delay and hope to have it up and running shortly.
