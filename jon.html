About Jon

Jon Gruber is one of the newest instructors at the fire arts center of chicago. His area of expertise is the art of armor forging. Once you see the incredible works created on his anvil you will surely want to line up for a class. Jon not only makes the works but also puts them to the test in medieval military re-enactments. His armor making classes are bound to be popular so be sure to sign up before too late. You don't want to miss out.
